The global tourism landscape is volatile. **PULSE.ai** transforms raw data into actionable foresight. Using simulated neural-prophet models, the platform allows policymakers to visualize demand heatmaps and deep-dive into specific destination trends.

### Key Modules:
* **Intelligence Dashboard:** A macro view of global tourism KPIs, growth metrics, and market heatmaps.
* **AI Forecast Engine:** Granular 12-month projections for specific cities (Tokyo, Paris, Bali, NYC) with toggleable travel types.
* **Risk Hub:** Real-time monitoring of infrastructure capacity and environmental impact risks.
